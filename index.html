About me

I'm a PhD Candidate in CASIA working in multi-agent reinforcement learning. I graduated from Beijing Jiaotong University in July 2019.

I am interested in probabilistic graphical model, generative model, contrastive learning and multi-agent reinforcement learning. Recently I worked on MARL applied on video game and route packeging.
